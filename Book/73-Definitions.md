# Appendix C: Definitions

Agile
: weapons suffer Disadvantage on Skirmish Action Rolls.

Defensive
: grants the item's wielder +1 Iron.

Non-lethal
: damage cannot take its target below 1 HP.

Powerful
: weapons suffer Disadvantage on Finesse Action Rolls.
